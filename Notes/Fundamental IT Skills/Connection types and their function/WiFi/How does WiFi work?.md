- WiFi uses radio waves  of 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz.
- The computers wireless adapter translates data into a radio signal  and after that transmits it using antenna.
-  A wireless router receives the signal and decodes after that the router sends the data to the internet trough wired Ethernet connection

- WiFi uses 802.11 networking standards which come in several types.



[[Common 802.11 Standards]]


**WiFi Hot Spots** - it's simply an area with an accessible wireless network. The term is most often used to refer to wireless networks in public areas like airports and coffee shops. Some are free and some require fees for use.
